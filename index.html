About Us

   Unfazed is an alternative rock trio hailing from Regina, SK Canada. The in-your-face rhythm sections of bands such as Nirvana and the twinkling emo winged leads of more present day acts like Mom Jeans and Modern Baseball served as influential building blocks for the group, eventually leading to the creation of the Unfazed sound; a specific brand of Post-punk that combines these elements with ease that can be easily recognized as an authentic voice of the Canadian Midwest.

Unfazed consists of Tanner Bolianatz on vocals and lead guitar, Bryson Bolianatz on rhythm guitar, and Drew Osborne on drums/percussion. Years of friendship and brotherhood have allowed each member to seamlessly blend their individual influences into the group, which creates an air of familiar nostalgia for the classic punk rock and grunge acts of the 1990s while s simultaneously providing a fresh and modernized sound that serves as a catalyst for a new generation of alternative rock.
